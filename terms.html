Terms & Conditions

1. Services We Provide

Cooperative Tech LLC offers digital and remote administrative services, including:

Digital & IT Services

  • Website design & development
  • Mobile app development
  • Indie gaming apps
  • E-commerce setup
  • SEO services
  • Google Ads & Facebook Ads management
  • PowerApps development
  • Power BI dashboards & reporting
  • RPA (automation)
  • IT consulting
  • Graphic design and branding

Trucking Dispatch & Remote Back-Office

  • Remote load monitoring
  • Load board management
  • Load booking based on carrier preferences
  • Back-office support (invoicing, emails, paperwork)
  • Broker communication
  • Route planning

Note: Cooperative Tech LLC is not a motor carrier and not a freight broker. All dispatching is administrative and performed remotely.

2. Pricing & Payments

All services offered by Cooperative Tech LLC are customised. We do not provide fixed pricing because each project and dispatch relationship is unique.

Before starting any work, clients receive:

  • A written quote
  • Pricing breakdown (percentage-based / weekly fees for dispatch)
  • Scope of work
  • Payment terms

No work begins until the client approves the price.

3. Delivery Policy

Digital Services

All deliverables (websites, apps, graphics, marketing, dashboards) are delivered:

  • By email
  • Online platforms
  • Cloud storage
  • Shared portals

Delivery timelines are shared during project scoping.

Dispatch Services

"Delivery" consists of:

  • Load search
  • Booking
  • Sending rate confirmations
  • Coordination with brokers
  • Planning routes

Completion depends on carrier acceptance and broker timelines.

4. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Digital Services

  • Full refund if project has not started
  • Partial refund if work is in progress
  • No refund after final delivery of work

Dispatch Services

  • No refunds after loads are booked
  • Carrier may cancel the dispatch agreement with 3-day notice
  • Dispatch fees for already-booked loads must be paid
