Services We Provide

Genzonix Dispatch LLC offers professional truck dispatching and digital services including:

Truck Dispatching & Back-Office Support

  • Load sourcing and booking from all major load boards
  • Broker negotiation and rate confirmation
  • Route planning and optimization
  • Back-office support (invoicing, paperwork, emails)
  • 24/7 dispatch support and check calls
  • Performance analytics and reporting

Digital & IT Services

  • Website design & development
  • Mobile app development
  • SEO, Google Ads & Facebook Ads management
  • Power BI dashboards & data analytics
  • RPA & process automation
  • IT consulting and graphic design

Note: Genzonix Dispatch LLC is not a motor carrier and not a freight broker. All dispatching services are administrative in nature and performed remotely.

Pricing & Payments

All services are customized per client. Before starting any work, clients receive:

  • A written quote with pricing breakdown
  • Clear scope of work and deliverables
  • Payment terms (percentage-based or weekly fees for dispatch)
  • Agreement on all terms before commencement

No work begins until the client approves the price and scope.

Delivery Policy

Dispatch Services

Service delivery includes load sourcing, booking, sending rate confirmations, broker coordination, and route planning. Completion timelines depend on carrier acceptance and broker schedules.

Digital Services

All digital deliverables (websites, apps, graphics, marketing materials, dashboards) are delivered electronically via email, cloud storage, or shared portals. Timelines are established during project scoping.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Dispatch Services

  • No refunds after loads are booked
  • Carrier may cancel the dispatch agreement with 3-day written notice
  • Dispatch fees for already-booked loads remain payable

Digital Services

  • Full refund if project has not yet started
  • Partial refund if work is in progress (based on completion)
  • No refund after final delivery of completed work
